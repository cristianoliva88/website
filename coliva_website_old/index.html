Research Interests

I am interested in survey sampling, nonparametric statistics, shape-restricted inference and extreme value analysis. Recently, I have been working on the development of new methodologies to analyze complex survey data that consider both the sampling structure and shape constraints, under the advisement of Dr. Mary Meyer and Dr. Jean Opsomer.

One of the things I enjoy the most of being a statistician is the interdisciplinary work with other areas. In that sense, I have offered statistical consulting to professionals in the fields of Biology, Animal Sciences, Geology, Soil and Crop Sciences, Psychology, among others. Feel free to contact me if you need some statistical advice.
